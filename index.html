How it works

One weekend. The whole program of action.

The Back to Basics format walks newcomers through all Twelve Steps in four one-hour sessions, exactly as it was done in the early days of recovery — straight from the Big Book, with no waiting and no mystery.

Session 01

We admit, believe, and decide

The newcomer faces the problem honestly and turns toward a Power greater than themselves.

Session 02

A searching, fearless inventory

Who we are

Dedicated to saving lives

The Back to Basics Foundation is dedicated to saving the lives of alcoholics, addicts, and the obsessive-compulsive by showing them the sheer simplicity of the original program of recovery — guiding them through the Twelve Steps as the pioneers took them in the 1940s.

We coordinate the efforts of beginners' meetings around the world, answer questions as they arise, follow up on Twelfth-Step calls, and conduct seminars and workshops based on the Back to Basics, Back to the Basics of Recovery, and How to Listen to God programs.

Our promise

Not a penny in royalties

Wally P. receives no income from any book, CD, or DVD he has produced. He insists this is his Twelfth-Step work, for which he cannot be paid.

Any proceeds, after expenses, go toward giving books at no cost to individuals and groups who cannot afford them.
